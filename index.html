About TON Blockchain

What is TON?

The Open Network (TON) is a decentralized blockchain designed to handle millions of transactions quickly and securely.

Key Features

Fast transactions, low fees, and scalable architecture make TON ideal for mass adoption.

Use Cases

Payments, decentralized apps, NFTs, and secure digital asset storage.

Wallet Security Best Practices

1

Use Official Wallets

Only download wallets from official sources like app stores or verified websites.

2

Secure Your Seed Phrase

Never share your recovery phrase and store it in a safe, offline location.

3

Enable 2FA

Use two-factor authentication for additional security layers.

4

Verify Transactions

Always double-check addresses and amounts before confirming transactions.
