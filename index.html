Wallet Security Fundamentals

Seed Phrase Protection

Your 24-word seed phrase is the master key to your wallet. Never share it with anyone.

  • Store offline in secure location
  • Never digitalize or photograph
  • Use metal backup solutions

Phishing Protection

Recognize and avoid phishing attempts targeting your cryptocurrency.

  • Verify website URLs carefully
  • Use bookmark for official sites
  • Enable 2FA when available

Hardware Wallets

Use hardware wallets for significant cryptocurrency holdings.

  • Private keys never leave device
  • Physical confirmation required
  • Immune to computer malware
